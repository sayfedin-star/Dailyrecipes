---
title: "Perfect Soft-Baked Chocolate Chip Cookies"
date: "2026-06-14"
description: "The ultimate recipe for soft-baked chocolate chip cookies that are chewy on the inside, crispy on the edges, and loaded with rich chocolate pools."
image: "../../assets/images/cookies.webp"
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 10
servings: 24
calories: 180
category: "Dessert"
tags: ["cookies", "chocolate", "baking", "sweet"]
ingredients:
  - "2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour"
  - "1/2 tsp baking soda"
  - "1/2 tsp salt"
  - "1 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled"
  - "1/2 cup white granulated sugar"
  - "1 cup packed brown sugar"
  - "1 tbsp vanilla extract"
  - "1 egg + 1 egg yolk (at room temperature)"
  - "2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks"
steps:
  - "Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper."
  - "In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside."
  - "In a large bowl, beat the melted butter, brown sugar, and white sugar together until smooth and well combined."
  - "Beat in the vanilla extract, whole egg, and egg yolk until the mixture is light and creamy."
  - "Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined. Do not overmix."
  - "Fold in the chocolate chunks or chips using a wooden spoon or spatula."
  - "Drop large spoonfuls of dough (about 2 tablespoons each) onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 2 inches of space between them."
  - "Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are still soft."
  - "Remove from the oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire cooling rack to cool completely."
---

These chocolate chip cookies are a household favorite. Using melted butter instead of softened butter ensures a much chewier texture, and adding an extra egg yolk binds the dough beautifully while adding rich moisture. Pair them with a cold glass of milk for the perfect afternoon treat!
